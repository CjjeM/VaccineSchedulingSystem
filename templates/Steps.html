HOW TO USE THE COVID-19 SCHEDULING SYSTEM

STEP 1: Create an Account!

Creating an account is needed to fully utilize the system. The needed details are as follows: Name, City, House Address, Date of Birth, Contact Number, Email Address, and Password.

The COVID-19 Vaccine Scheduling System: Step 1 of 4

STEP 2: Login!

After creating an account, you may now login using your registered email address and password. Then, you can now make an appointment and view your appointment details.

The COVID-19 Vaccine Scheduling System: Step 2 of 4

STEP 3: Find Your Vaccination Site and Make an Appointment

With the help of your registered house address and city, the map will show all of the nearest vaccination sites and even suggest. Below the suggested vaccination sites, you can see the vaccination sites appointments. This allows you to choose your preferred vaccination sites, vaccine, date of appointment, and the type of vaccination. Click on Make Appointment for finalization.

The COVID-19 Vaccine Scheduling System: Step 3 of 4

STEP 4: View your Appointment Details

After making an appointment, you may now view it via our system and at the same time you will receive an SMS notification regarding your appointment details.

The COVID-19 Vaccine Scheduling System: Step 4 of 4
